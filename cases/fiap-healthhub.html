01 / THE CONTEXT

The Disconnection in Healthcare

In Brazil's public health system (SUS), scheduling appointments for long-term treatments is a bureaucratic nightmare. Patients often miss critical dates due to lack of reminders, while clinics suffer from high no-show rates.

Our challenge during the FIAP Startup Challenge was to create a digital solution that bridged this gap within 3 weeks.

02 / DISCOVERY

Mapping the Patient Journey

We interviewed 10 patients and 3 clinic administrators. The core insight was surprising: it wasn't that patients didn't want to go, it was that the physical appointment cards were often lost or damaged.

03 / THE SOLUTION

A Unified Digital Hub

We designed HealthHub: a centralized app that digitizes the "Caderneta de Vacinação" (Vaccination Card) and automates reminders via WhatsApp integration.

Automated Reminders

Instead of relying on push notifications (which users often disable), we integrated with WhatsApp API to send reminders 24h before the appointment.

Digital History

A permanent, cloud-based record of all procedures, accessible via QR Code for doctors, ensuring data portability.

04 / THE IMPACT

Results & Takeaways

The prototype was pitched to a panel of investors and health professionals, securing 2nd place in the Startup Challenge. The feedback highlighted the scalability of the WhatsApp integration as a key differentiator.

LESSON LEARNED

"Accessibility isn't just about contrast ratios; it's about meeting users where they already are. WhatsApp was a better channel than a standalone app notification."
